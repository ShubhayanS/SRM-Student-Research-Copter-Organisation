Mission

The Team has brought in laurels to the University by turning it to reality which was once just a dream. The Team has participated in various National and International competition and has always earned a platform of dignity and appreciation for its hard work and dedication. The Team pursues its motto to inspire others with its hard work, initiate what were once ideas, and innovate to make the world an easier and developed place to live.

Here at Team SCRO we believe in converting dreams into reality. We take pride in winning various competitions and project exhibitions and conducting air shows at various Universities along with extensive Research work in the field of copters, continuously developing them to satisfy present needs. The journey through the 4 years has been phenomenal and the team has elevated from the idea of two budding enthusiasts into one of the most established and successful teams of the University
