SOME OTHER Missions

INTROMET'14

The Indian economy is basically driven by agriculture, which in turn, depends upon the vagaries of monsoons. Thus, understanding the underlying physical processes, monitoring, and predicting their behavior, etc. are highly challenging areas of research for atmospheric scientists. The Indian Meteorological Society (IMS) intends to organize an international symposium, INTROMET – 2014 (International Tropical Metrology) on Monsoons Observations, Prediction, and Sustainability (MOPS). Thus, we introduced Autonomous Ambient Weather Monitoring Drone (AAWMD) designed, fabricated, tested and demonstrated in the International Metrology Conference Held in Chennai on February 21, 2014.

INTERNATIONAL MICRO AERIAL VEHICLE - IMAV 2014

The IMAV is a yearly event that combines a scientific conference with a technological competition involving Micro Air Vehicles (MAVs). Such a combination allows research groups from all over the world to share their knowledge, and stimulates them to focus on research that can be used in real life scenarios. The team showed its potential in the event.

DST INSPIRE CAMP 2018

Team SCRO came forward to share their knowledge with the school children on copters and it was a successful event with the interactive session among all. It was held from 9th January till 13th January to embrace the knowledge about unmanned aerial vehicles.

WILD LIFE CONSERVATION UNMANNED AERIAL VEHICLE




WCUAVC 2014

The Wildlife Conservation UAV Challenge challenged the world to develop unmanned aircraft able fight day and night, independent of weather, and with a degree of autonomy well beyond that of military aircraft. Our goal is find a location which simulates the terrain, topography, and temperature.

SOUTH INDIA SCIENCE FAIR 2014

The team demonstrated the drone Q 2, Rudra T1 and the albatross models in the SISF 2014 held at MCC Higher Secondary School, Chetpet, Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India to the Education minister of Tamil Nadu and several other dignitaries to motivate young talent into the field of UAV’s.
• Drona Q1, Q2 - The Autonomous Aerial Quad fabricated and tested by the members of team SCRO during November 13, 2013.

REGIONAL SCIENCE CONGRESS 2013

• Ashwin & Astra - delta wing, flat base dihedral depron models.

TAMIL NADU SCIENCE FAIR 2013

Jawaharlal Nehru Tamil Nadu science expo held at the Jaigopal Garodia National Higher Secondary School on December 5, 2013. It was a three-day event where the members of team SCRO demonstrated the multi rotor models to the students of various districts of Tamil Nadu. Almost 1 lakh children attended the event.

• Albatross - Trainer stick plane for piloting practice.

• Vaamana M1- Mini Quad weighing 196g with indoor maneuvering capabilities.
